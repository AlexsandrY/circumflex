Some SmartyPants stuff
===============

* copyright sign: (c), (C);
* registered trademark sign: (r), (R);
* trademark sign: (tm), (TM);
* curly quotes: "this is some text"; и с "юникодом" тоже;
* dash: --;
* horizontal ellipsis: ...;
* ampersand wrapped into `span` for extra styling: &;
* arrows: <- and ->.
